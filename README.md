The Air Quality Prediction & Analysis project focuses on analyzing environmental air quality data to understand pollution patterns and identify trends across different locations across Indian . The primary objective is to clean, preprocess, and explore the dataset using Python to uncover meaningful insights about air pollutants and their distribution.

This project applies Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and statistical techniques to examine pollutant concentrations, compare air quality across cities and states, and visualize environmental trends through informative charts and graphs. By analyzing key air quality indicators, the project helps identify pollution hotspots and provides a better understanding of factors affecting air quality.

The analysis serves as a foundation for future predictive modeling, enabling the development of machine learning solutions for forecasting air quality and supporting data-driven environmental decision-making.
